# BinGo Product Definition

## Vision
To incentivize responsible waste management through an AI-powered gamified platform.

## Core Features
- **AI Waste Scan:** Classify items and receive preparation steps.
- **Gamification:** Earn points and badges for scanning and recycling correctly.
- **Education:** Daily tips and a database for waste disposal information.
- **Leaderboard:** Compete with others on impact stats.

## Key Goals
- Secure user accounts and data.
- High-performance multimodal AI processing.
- Scalable and maintainable codebase.
